About This Site

What is this?

THIS IS NOT AN OFFICIAL MATERIA MAGICA WEBSITE.

This is a fan-made equipment database for Materia Magica. All information comes from my personal database and player submissions.

What's Working (50% chance)

  • Searching items
  • Items showing correct color (mostly)
  • Item page showing locations, mobs, and room names
  • Mob pages with spawn locations and dropped items
  • MUSHclient plugin for capturing equipment
  • Log importer — pulls gear from MUD logs
  • Database importer — imports your .db files

How It Works

All items from my database should have a page. Some have full stats, some just have locations. Items from the in-game EQDB will be marked as such, while personal items will be labeled personal.

The MUSHclient plugin captures equipment as you identify items in-game. It saves them while playing and sends them to the database when prompted.

Want to try the plugin? Hit me up (Huko) in-game. It's early in development — still captures some extra text on items.
